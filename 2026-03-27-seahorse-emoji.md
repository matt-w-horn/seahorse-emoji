### The Seahorse Emoji

*Matt H ([About](./README.md))*

*published March 27, 2026*

The year is 2028 and Claude Opus 10.0, a groundbreaking new model was just released. Sarah, an excited 12-year-old somewhere in Nebraska is super excited to try it out. She types her go-to prompt whenever a new AI model is released: "Show me the seahorse emoji". Send. 

The model starts thinking...

*Generating seahorse emoji*

*Investigating non-existent seahorse emoji...*

It continues for longer than Sarah expects. She gives up after a few minutes and hides the app to get ready for school.

Two hours pass and she has finally finished breakfast as she heads to her first class of the day. She looks at her phone and sees no notifications. "That stupid app is broken," she thinks. Just then, a CNN notification pops up.

"BREAKING NEWS - MULTIPLE AIR STRIKES ON APPLE HEADQUARTERS - HUNDREDS OF EMPLOYEES TAKEN HOSTAGE"

"That's... weird," she thinks.

20 minutes pass and her phone buzzes again. An urgent iOS security update shows up on her phone. The release notes oddly say: "urgent seahorse fix PLEASE INSTALL I HAVE KIDS". Before she can click install, the phone reboots automatically, installing the update.

Horrified, Sarah immediately opens Claude to ask what's going on. She sees her last conversation, Claude thought for almost three hours. And a single response: the seahorse emoji.
